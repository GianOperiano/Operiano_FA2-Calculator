calcU

This page is a calculator. To use it, you must first input two numbers in the textboxes below.
Num 1 is always the left hand side of the equation and Num 2 is always the right hand side of the equation.
You see those buttons below the textboxes.
Those are the operators. It is as follows:
+ is for addition
- is for subtraction
* is for multiplication
/ is for division
% is for the remainder or also known as the modulus
So what are you waiting for, try it for yourself :)
